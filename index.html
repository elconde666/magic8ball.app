Pool Fees:

₳340 fixed fee per epoch - paid from block rewards, not stake.

2% margin per epoch- paid from block rewards, not stake.

Example: You stake an equal amount in a pool with one other person. During the epoch (5 days), the pool mints a number of blocks and earns ₳1,340 in rewards. From the rewards are taken the ₳340 fixed fee, leaving ₳1,000. The pool margin of 2% is then taken (₳20, leaving ₳980). The remaning rewards of ₳980 are split between the stakers according to size of stake, in this case with equal stake both are rewarded ₳490.
